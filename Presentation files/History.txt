1962 Brian David Josephson produces the mathematics of the Josephson effect.

1999 the D-Wave company is created based on the quantum annealing theory developed by Tadashi Kadowaki, Hidetoshi Nishimori at Tokyo Institute of Technology. 

Hidetoshi Nishimori said “When I proposed the concept of quantum annealing, I never expected that someone will manufacture such a machine, It’s a crazy idea, but they did it and they sold it”. 

Actually D-Wave sold their first system for 10M to Lockheed Martin in 2010.
